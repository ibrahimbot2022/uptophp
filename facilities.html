Facilities



Lending


The college library circulates books and other reading resources.




Reference


College Library has got the resourceful collection consists of Encyclopedias, Dictionaries, Handbooks, and Core Engineering subjects through which library servers both Short and Long range Reference Services.




Online Public Access Cataloguing (OPAC)


Here the users can access the reading resources by consulting OPAC on Author, Title, and Subject.



Career Information Services


The Library also maintains information about Professional careers in the form of providing University Catalogues, Brochures, Bulletins and Institutional Handouts.



Photocopying Services


Library provides photocopies of Journal Articles and useful information to the Student and Faculty.



E-Journals / E-Books Access


All e-Journals & e-Books are made available throughout the campus via Intranet.




Referral Service


The needy users requires more specialized information and have been referred to use other reputed libraries like National Digital Library, IISc, IIM, British Library, CMTI, NAL, ISRO etc.
